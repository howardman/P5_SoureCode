About Us

It is very important to do a feasibility study before starting this project. People nowadays in the 21st century love shopping online. Having the ability to shop for groceries online is very convenient and preferable for many people who have very busy daily schedules. Some people concerned about shopping online for groceries are concerned because they want to know things like the expiration date, where the product is from, whether the product is non-GMO, etc. On our website, our goal is to make sure that the customers are able to know all these things before purchasing a product.

Our vision

The users of this website will be categorized into three different types: visitors, registered members, and administrators. Visitors will be able to view all the publicly obtainable features of the site which will include the ability to browse products, view the details of products, and look other content of site.

Our mission

The aim of this website is to create a user friendly website for customers and make it easy for them to buy our products. We want our customers to feel like shopping on our website is just as simple as physically going to the local grocery store.

Behind the brand

As we mentioned above, some of our main products will be like vegetables, meat and fruits. Those products all have the same issue, which is they’re hard to store and can deteriorate quickly. So, unlike clothes and electronic stores, our return policy will be more strict.

Our Start

Since our grocery store is online based, it is absolutely important to either build or cooperate with local transportation companies. Our main products would be vegetables, meat, fruits, etc., which generally have a heavier weight and bigger volume compared to other items people purchase online such as clothes.
